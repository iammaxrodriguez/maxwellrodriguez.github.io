Work

Above is a logo of the company I have interned at the past two summers, Acme Communications INC. For my two summers here my official title was an Network Engineer Intern. Meeting the team on the first Day was a joyful experience and I knew I would fit in well there. It's a Network company in Michigan City IN, that uses fiber optical cables to connect homes to the fastest wifi/internet available. I worked on splicing the fiber optic cables into the homes and did a lot of labor to see the actual process of how the internet worked. A lot of hard work goes into the process and by the end of my first summer, we connected ten different schools to our computer network servers.

Leading into the second summer I started in the same role but was actually able to update and see the servers that we set up. I would upgrade firmware and work in the UNIX system. I also worked with switches, connecting the correct ports with the cables to ensure network. Switches match the destination address or addresses and sends the information out through the appropriate ports that leads to the destination devices.
